Entry Requirements

To gain admission to BTTI, one must satisfy any of the following minimum requirements:

  • Professional Courses: KCSE Qualification (or equivalent examination) with an aggregate of C+.
  • Diploma: KCSE Qualification (or equivalent examination) with an aggregate C- (Minus) and above.
  • Certificate: KCSE Qualification (or equivalent examination) with an aggregate D Plain and above.
  • Artisan Course: Holder of KCSE or KCPE Certificate.
  • Vocational Certificate: Ideal for fresh high school graduates and established professionals in various areas.
